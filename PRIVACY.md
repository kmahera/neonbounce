# Privacy Policy for Neon Bounce

**Effective date:** 14/07/2026

This privacy policy explains how the Neon Bounce mobile game ("the App") handles information.

## Summary

Neon Bounce does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App works
entirely offline and has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party
data sharing.

## Information We Store

The App saves a small amount of data **only on your device**, using local storage that never
leaves your phone:

- Your high score
- Your selected character color
- Your selected game speed (Slow / Normal / Fast)
- Your sound on/off preference

This data is not linked to your identity, is never transmitted anywhere, and is not accessible
to us or anyone else. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes this data.

## Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, request, or transmit:

- Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking or advertising
- Photos, contacts, or other device content
- Any data to third-party servers, analytics platforms, or advertising networks

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13,
because it does not collect personal information from any user.

## Permissions

The App may request standard permissions used by its underlying technology framework (such as
network access used by development tooling). These are not used to collect or transmit any
personal information during normal use of the App.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised
effective date.
